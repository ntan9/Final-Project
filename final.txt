Bill's Adventures v. 1.0 

Made by Nick Tan, Jacob Yoo, and Jason Chen using the arcade library by Paul Vincent Craven.


WALKTHROUGH (How to obtain all keys):
1. Search the bed
2. Grab the crowbar and smash the crate
3. The answer to the lever puzzle is "Neutral", "Left", "Left", "Right"
4. Grab the stick in the maze and fix the lever
5. The answer to the other lever puzzle is "Right", "Left", "Right", "Left", "Neutral"
6. Leave the maze to win!
